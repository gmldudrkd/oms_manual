# Approve / Reject Permissions (Administrator)

Administrators (Admin) review the permissions requested by other users and either approve or reject them.

:::warning A Task That Carries Responsibility
Permission approval is **recorded as the responsibility of the approving administrator**. Always confirm that the requester is someone who should have access to the data in question.
:::

---

## How to Process

1. In [User List](./user-list), click the **Email** of the user you want to process to open their detail page.
2. Review the contents of each request in the **Permission Requests** table.
   - Requested Date, Brand, Corp, Role, Requested Reason, Status
3. For requests with a status of **AWAITING**, choose **"Approve"** or **"Reject"**.

### Approve

- In the confirmation dialog, review the notice *"...This action will be recorded as your responsibility..."* and confirm with **"Approve"**.
- Once approved, the requester can immediately access the data for that brand and corporation.

### Reject

- In the confirmation dialog, review the notice *"...This will permanently delete the data."* and confirm with **"Reject"**.
- The data for a rejected request is deleted.

---

## Sign-Up Approval

If a new registrant's **Approval Status** is **AWAITING**, their sign-up has not yet been approved. You must approve the sign-up itself on the user detail page before they can log in and use permissions.
