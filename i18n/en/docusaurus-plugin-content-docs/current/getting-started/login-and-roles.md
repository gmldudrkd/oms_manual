# Login and Permissions

## Logging In

1. When you go to the OMS web address, the Sign In screen appears.
2. Enter your **company email** and password, then log in.
3. On successful login, you are taken to the [Order Dashboard](../order/dashboard) screen.

:::note Don't have an account?
If you are using the system for the first time, you must first create an account via **Sign Up**. After signing up, you need an administrator's **approval** and **permission grant** before you can see any data.
:::

---

## No permissions means no data appears

If you can log in but the order list is empty, it most likely means you **do not have access permissions yet**. The OMS only shows you data for the **brand × corporation** scope assigned to you.

To obtain permissions, you must submit a **Request Permission** and wait for an administrator's approval. (The request screen and process are explained in detail in [User — Request Permission](../user/request-permission).)

---

## Role Types

Even for the same data, what you can do depends on your **role**. Permissions are granted separately for each brand × corporation combination, so your role may differ by combination — for example, "Manager for GM KR, None for GM JP."

| Role | Display | What you can do (overview) |
|------|-----------|---------------------|
| **Admin** | Blue chip | All operational tasks within the scope + management such as approving user permissions |
| **Manager** | Green chip | General operational processing such as orders, returns, exchanges, and stock |
| **Request** | Orange chip | A permission has been requested and is awaiting approval |
| **None** | Gray chip | No permission (cannot view) |

:::tip
You can check your current permissions by opening your own account in the [User](../user/user-list) list. It shows at a glance which role you hold for each brand and corporation.
:::

---

## Logging Out

Click the **account menu (person icon + your ID)** in the top-right corner of the screen, then select **"Log out"** to sign out.
